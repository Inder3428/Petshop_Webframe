The Ocelot

The ocelot is a medium-sized spotted wild cat that tends to be solitary and territorial. It is efficient at climbing, leaping and swimming. It preys on small terrestrial mammals, such as armadillos, opossums, and lagomorphs. Although not your normal house cat, many ocelots turn out to be incredibly affectionate and make loving companions, even if they are extremely needy.

Pomeranian

Pomeranians are known for being smart, curious, energetic, feisty, and bold. They are typically very playful and love to be the center of attention. They can make great pets for families but may not be the best choice for those who have small children.

Budgie Birds

The budgie and other parakeet species are native to Australia. These wild species, however, are slightly smaller than the birds normally found in pet stores, which have now undergone decades of captive breeding.
